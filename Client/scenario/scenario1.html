Scenario 1

Lorenzo sits at his desk in his home studio. He opens a trusted plant website to find a new addition.

The homepage features calming images of lush plants. He feels drawn in by the variety available.

Lorenzo sees a prompt for a Plant Personality Survey. It promises to help him find the perfect plant, so he decides to take it.

The survey inquires about his style. Lorenzo opts for calming, which matches his studio vibe.

Next, it asks how much time he can spend on plant care. He chooses minimal maintenance due to his busy schedule.

The survey asks about his environment. He selects low light since his studio has small windows.

The survey recommends a Peace Lily. It thrives in low light and symbolizes peace, making it a great fit for Lorenzo.

Lorenzo clicks on the 360-degree view of the Peace Lily. He imagines how it will look on his desk.

He browses decorative pots and picks a matte clay pot. Its earthy tone suits his studio aesthetic.

Before leaving, he sets up delivery notifications. He also requests care tips via email and adds the delivery date to his calendar.

Happy with his choice, Lorenzo clicks Buy Now. He confirms his shipping details and adds a personal note.

While hovering over the plant, the site plays nature sounds. The rustling leaves remind him of his music.
